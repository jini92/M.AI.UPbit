# Tistory Representative Image Strategy - M.AI.UPbit

- **Visual style**: title-only cover with a calm quant dashboard mood. No coin logos collage, no noisy charts.
- **Palette**: deep navy `#0F172A`, electric blue `#2563EB`, mint accent `#14B8A6`, white text `#F8FAFC`.
- **Ratio**: 16:9 base (`1280x720`). Safe title area centered within the middle 70% width.
- **Typography**: one bold Korean title line + one small subtitle/date line. Keep to 2 weights max, high contrast only.
- **Reuse vs custom**:
  - Reuse the same template for routine newsletter numbers (#1, #2, weekly signals).
  - Use a custom cover only for milestone posts (major backtest result, product launch, strategy deep dive).
